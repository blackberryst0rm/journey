What is a White Water Kayak?

Oh the thrill! White water Kayaks are the Kayaks you want if you are planning to take on heavy rapids! These agile, reslient Kayaks are designs for water that is moving so fast and against so many obsticles that the water turns white from the turbulence. These Kayaks do some in multiple sub flavors. Though, you will find the most common ones, river runners, to be about 10 feet long with an agressive forward position and high tracking point behavor. This style of Kayakingis dangerous.
