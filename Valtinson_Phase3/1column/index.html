What is Kayaking?

Kayaking is one of the most popular and currently active hobbies one can have! As an activity Kayaking uses a Canoe style water craft with a paddle to propel you through the water. It has fantastic health benfits as well as the potential to expose you to new experiences, places and people. Lets start by taking a look at the types of kayaking that can be done! There are multiple typs of Kayaking. In-fact there are at least five kinds of Kayaks one could use to enjoy the hobby. Below are the five types!

  • Recreational
  • Sit-on-Top
  • Inflatable
  • Touring
  • White water

Finally, in the menu you can find out more about each type!
