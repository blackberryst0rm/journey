What is a Touring Kayak?

Touring Kayaks! These Kayaks are for the big Adventures requiring long distance and possibly unstable waters. You will find they have keel, and longer tapered bow and stern. Think fishing or exploring uncommon places! Also, they make a great vessel for relaxing the day away as well due to their length being above average.

  • great for long journeys
  • long and thin overall
  • Hard to sink, goes over obsticles
  • Used for fishing
  • Great for lots of environments
